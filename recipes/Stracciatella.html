Stracciatella soup



Ingredients:

  • Broth
  • 4 eggs
  • 50gr parmesan
  • 50 gr breadcrumbs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Lemon zest
  • Nutmeg


Steps:

  1. Mix the eggs in a bowl, adding the parmesan, the breadcrumbs, the lemon zest, the nutemeg, and finally the salt and the pepper.
  2. Bring the broth just shy of a boil.
  3. Stream the eggs into the hot broth.
  4. Let the egg mixture cook for 5 min.
  5. Serve the soup piping hot.
