Spaghetti al pomodoro



Ingredients:

  • 320 gr spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • salt to taste


Steps:

  1. Finely dice the onion.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan over a medium heat. Put the onion into the heated pan and sweat it.
  3. When the onion is translucent, add the tomatoes. Let the sauce cook and reduce for 15 min.
  4. Cook the spaghetti in plenty of boiling salted water according to the package instructions.
  5. When the pasta is just shy of al dente, drain the pasta and mix it with the sauce.
  6. If you like it, tear a bunch of basil into the pasta.
  7. Serve the pasta piping hot and enjoy.
